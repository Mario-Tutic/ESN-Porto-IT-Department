ESNcard

 
 

What is the ESNcard? The ESNcard is the official membership card of the Erasmus Student Generation. It's a proof of membership in an ESN section and indirectly of the ESNNetwork

What is it for? The ESNcard allows you to:
  • Join all of our events at ESN Porto (including trips, cultural events, parties and sports events)
  • Use all the discounts from our partners in the city (including surf classes, bars, housing, communication, transportation, ...)
  • Access to all the events and discounts in all of the sections of the network (+500 sections in over 42 countries in Europe)

Go to esncard.org or our Partners page to check for discounts!

 
 
 
 

The ESNcard is also accepted as a valid student ID in many others businesses in Porto, since ESN is an organization recognized by the European Union. The card is valid for one year, so you can freely use it after you go back home or while you are travelling across Europe

Who can get the ESNcard? You can get the ESNcard, if you belong to one of the following groups:
  • Erasmus+ students
  • Erasmus+ trainees
  • Erasmus+ Full Internation Degree incoming students
  • Internation incoming students or trainees on a mobility programme, other than Erasmus+
  • International undergraduate or postgraduate full degree students
  • EVS participants
  • ESNers: either active on the Local, National or International Level. They are all members of a section

How to get the ESNcard?

  • Fill in the form GET YOURS!
  • Come to our office and bring: Photo (ID/Card size) and 20?
  • And that's it! Be part of the Erasmus Generation!

After getting your card, register it in ESNcard.org with just a few steps and get access to an internation level of a partnerships and discounts!
